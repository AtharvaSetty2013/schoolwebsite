Campus Life

School Church

Was Establised in 1911

St. Peter's Chapel was dedicated in the year 1911 and since then it continues to inspire several hundreds of students who enter the portals of the school.



School Library

Was Establised in 1989

A gateway to knowledge, the school library, “Pettigrew Resource Centre” is truly an invaluable asset to the students and the staff in the teaching-learning process.



Auditorium

Was Establised in 1987

Standing on the erstwhile Third eleven field, is now a state of the art Auditorium The Bishop Edward Lynch Cotton Auditorium. It is fondly referred to as the “Great Cottonian Globe”.

  • International Yoga Day

    Cottons observes International Yoga Day every year to raise awareness about this ancient practice and to celebrate the physical and spiritual benefits that yoga brings to the world. The practice of yoga enhances physical, mental, and emotional health. It plays an important role in relaxing the mind and body and boosting people’s immune systems. Today, the cadets of Uniformed Services celebrated Yoga Day with great zeal and enthusiasm. Kumar and the masters in charge of uniformed services encouraged the cadets to participate eagerly in the celebration.
